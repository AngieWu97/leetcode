571.(Hard) 给定数字的频率查询中位数

Numbers
+----------+-------------+
|  Number  |  Frequency  |
+----------+-------------|
|  0       |  7          |
|  1       |  1          |
|  2       |  3          |
|  3       |  1          |
+----------+-------------+
000000012223

output
+--------+
| median |
+--------|
| 0.0000 |
+--------+

又是Median， T - T

SELECT AVG(a.number) median
FROM Numbers a
WHERE a.frequency >= ABS(
    (SELECT SUM(frequency) FROM Numbers WHERE a.number <= number) - 
    (SELECT SUM(frequency) FROM Numbers WHERE a.number >= number))
